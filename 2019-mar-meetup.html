March 2019 Meetup

Tue 26 March 2019 by Martin

Category: meetings

Date: March 26, 7 to 9pm
Venue: Zendesk, 401 Commonwealth Drive, #07-01 (map).
Signup: meetup page

58 attendees gathered at the Zendesk office and were surprised by once again unusual but awesome food choices. Of course, our speakers were great, too:

  • Entangled in Dependencies - Pipenv & Poetry by Alex Khaerov

The second talk was a hands-on demonstration about simple IoT applications using Raspberry Pi by Ang Swee Chye. This demonstration did not use any slides.
